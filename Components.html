    • Key Components of Our Humanoid Robot

    ESP32-CAM

    This module powers the robot’s vision, enabling image capture and real-time video streaming for object and face recognition tasks.

    Servos

    High-torque servos provide the joint movement, giving the robot human-like motion control for limbs and head rotation.

    Servo Driver

    This driver controls multiple servos simultaneously, ensuring smooth and coordinated movement of the robot’s limbs.

    Lithium Battery

    The power source of the robot, providing the necessary energy to run all components for extended periods of operation.

    Jumper Wires

    Essential for connecting all electronic components, jumper wires ensure reliable connections between sensors, drivers, and controllers.
