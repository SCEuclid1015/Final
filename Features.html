    Real-time Video Streaming and Object Recognition

    The humanoid robot employs the ESP32-CAM module, enabling it to capture live video streams and perform real-time object recognition. This capability allows the robot to interpret its surroundings, making it effective for tasks requiring visual feedback and interaction, such as identifying and responding to human gestures or objects within its environment.

    Human-like Movement with High Precision

    The robot is equipped with high-torque servos, which facilitate smooth and precise movements. These servos allow the robot to replicate complex human actions, such as walking, bending, and reaching. This human-like mobility enhances its ability to perform tasks that require dexterity, making it suitable for applications in various fields like rehabilitation or education.

    AI-Powered Interaction

    The integration of advanced artificial intelligence enables the humanoid robot to learn from interactions and adapt its behavior accordingly. This means the robot can engage users in dynamic conversations, adjust its actions based on user feedback, and improve its performance over time, making it an invaluable tool in interactive settings.

    Efficient Power Management

    The humanoid robot is powered by a lithium battery that is designed for longevity and efficiency. This power source allows the robot to operate for extended periods without frequent recharging, making it suitable for long-duration tasks in diverse environments, from classrooms to healthcare facilities.
